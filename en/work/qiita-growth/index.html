Case Study

Growing a Tech Blog to 1.43M Views

2019–present

Reached 1.43M cumulative views on Qiita through a deliberate growth strategy, growing daily views 7×

QiitaSEOAnalyticsTech writing

Background

I run a tech blog to share knowledge and build my personal brand. It stood at 400K views in November 2024 and needed a deliberate push to grow.

Constraints

  • Balancing with a full-time job (limited writing time)
  • A crowded tech-blog market
  • Sustaining a consistent publishing cadence

Approach

Focused on high-demand topics such as AI and cloud, identified through trend analysis. Applied thorough SEO (titles, tags, structure) and aimed for one high-quality article per day.

Implementation

Used analytics to study what made articles popular and fed it back into the writing. Prioritized reader relationships through comment responses and feedback.

Results

  • 1.43M cumulative views (+1M in 8 months)
  • ~5,000 daily views (7× growth)
  • 270+ articles published
  • Top article on 'Deep Research' exceeded 250K views and 100 likes

Learnings

Consistency matters most in technical writing. Combining timely coverage of trends with genuinely deep explanations is what earns reader trust.
