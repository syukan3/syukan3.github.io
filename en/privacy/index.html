Privacy Policy

How personal information is handled on this site (https://syukan3.github.io/).
Effective date: June 12, 2026

Information collected

  • Contact form: your name, email address, and message
  • Access data: page URLs, browser and device information, and cookie identifiers (collected automatically by the analytics tools described below)

Purpose of use

Personal information submitted through the contact form is used only to respond to your inquiry and for any necessary follow-up. It is never used for other purposes, and it is not shared with third parties without your consent except where required by law.

Third-party services

This site uses the following external services, which receive certain information from your browser when you visit.

  • Google Analytics (Google LLC) — used to understand how the site is used. Page URLs, browser and device information, and cookie identifiers are sent. To opt out, use the opt-out browser add-on. See the Google Privacy Policy for details.
  • reCAPTCHA Enterprise (Google LLC) — used on the contact page to prevent spam and abuse. Your IP address and browser information are sent. The Google Privacy Policy and Terms of Service apply.
  • Google Fonts (Google LLC) — used to serve web fonts. Your IP address is sent when fonts are fetched.

Data retention

Inquiry messages are kept only for as long as needed to handle them, and are deleted appropriately once no longer required.

Disclaimer

This site is not responsible for the handling of personal information on external sites linked from here; that is the responsibility of each site's operator.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be revised without notice as needed. Revisions take effect when posted on this page.
